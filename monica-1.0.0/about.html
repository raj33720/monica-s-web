About Hi, I'm Monica

Bridging technology with human narrative to craft meaningful solutions.

A self-made entrepreneur, founder of a global CMMI Level 3 tech company, and a published author. I believe technology is a human story. With over 15 years of experience, I transform complex business challenges into high-performance digital solutions while leading with vision, strategy, and a passion for authentic storytelling.

How I Got Here

My path began as a fresh IT graduate and an introverted bookworm. A pivotal win in an inter-school dance competition sparked a transformation, teaching me the power of expression. This journey of self-discovery continued through grooming classes and a mentor who saw my potential as a communicator, pushing me into public speaking and sales roles. My fascination with International Relationship Management led me to found MaMo TechnoLabs LLP in 2014. Today, I wear many hats from Developer and Product MVP Specialist to Delivery Head and Public Speaker guiding 20+ international client accounts annually and turning technological vision into market-ready strategy.

My Values & Beliefs

Technology as a Human Story

I believe the most impactful tech solutions are built on understanding human needs, emotions, and narratives. Every line of code should serve a purpose in the user's story.

Transformative Growth

From introvert to entrepreneur, developer to leader, I champion continuous learning and the courage to reinvent oneself. Growth happens outside comfort zones.

Strategic Partnership

Success is built on strong relationships. I focus on understanding core business challenges to become a strategic partner, not just a service provider, for global clients.

Authentic Expression

Whether leading a team, crafting a product, or writing a novel like "Arjun and The Women Who Waited," I value honest expression and sharing genuine insights from the journey.

Why Work With Me

You gain a partner who combines deep technical acumen with sharp market strategy. I specialize in de-risking innovation through a proven Product MVP approach, ensuring we build what truly matters. With hands-on experience from coding to C-suite leadership, I bridge the gap between vision and execution, delivering scalable, high-performance solutions.

I independently manage complex international accounts, ensuring clear communication, strategic alignment, and measurable results. My leadership philosophy is built on mentorship, transparency, and fostering teams that solve technological challenges with a constructive, collaborative mindset.

A Few More Words About Myself

My entrepreneurial spirit finds its parallel in creative writing as a published author of evocative titles. Recognized by platforms like Radio Mirchi, I see narrative as a core tool for connection in software, leadership, and life. I share candid takeaways from my wins and mistakes to inspire others to bridge their own gaps between ambition and reality. When not strategizing or writing, you might find me exploring a new leadership concept or drawing inspiration from the world's diverse stories.
