About Monica


Monica is an author and storyteller whose work reimagines epic narratives through the voices of women, silence, and emotional depth. Drawing inspiration from the Mahabharata, her writing explores love, sacrifice, dignity, and the unseen strength that shapes destinies. Her books blend lyrical prose with timeless myth to create intimate, reflective storytelling.
